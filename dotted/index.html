THE PROBLEM

The advant of blockchain propelled the financial indsutry into the realm of electronic, distributed currencies. While Bitcoin is a technology owned and operated by a group of developers federated via an open-source model, Central Banks can benefit from the power of virtual currencies, without losing the control that they need to develop monetary policies and ensure the safety of its citizens. Dotted Square offers a flexible technology that enables transactions to be fully traceabile, compliant with local and global regulations and laws, without zero compromise on privacy.

Blockchain Ledgers The Missing Link
Anonymous, untraceable transaction. Protocol supported and regulated by central banks. Identity supported as a core part of the protocol. Transaction are fully traceable and auditable.
Borderless transactions. AML and KYC support. All transactions are traceable with appropriate legal approvals.
Monetary policies are disconnected from market needs. Money supply issued and existing on the ledger is controlled and capped. Additions and removals from money supply are only made by the Central Bank.
